## App Name
Carrel - Paper Gallery

## Short Description (80 characters max)
Preview and organize your LaTeX papers from GitHub and GitLab repositories.

## Full Description (4000 characters max)
Carrel is your personal gallery for academic papers and LaTeX documents. Connect your GitHub or GitLab repositories and instantly preview PDFs from your research projects.

**Key Features:**

• **Sync from Git** - Connect GitHub, GitLab, or Overleaf repositories and automatically sync your PDF papers

• **Beautiful Gallery** - View all your papers in a clean, Pinterest-style gallery with thumbnails

• **Quick Preview** - Tap any paper to view the full PDF with smooth scrolling and zooming

• **Stay Organized** - Add titles and notes to your papers for easy reference

• **Share Papers** - Generate public links to share your research with colleagues

• **Real-time Sync** - Changes to your repositories appear instantly across all devices

• **Offline Access** - Downloaded PDFs are cached for offline viewing

• **Dark Mode** - Easy on your eyes during late-night research sessions

**Perfect for:**
- Researchers managing multiple papers across repositories
- Students organizing thesis chapters and drafts
- Academics sharing preprints with collaborators
- Anyone who works with LaTeX documents in Git

**Privacy-focused:**
Your papers are only accessible by you. OAuth tokens are encrypted and stored securely. We never share your data with third parties.

**Sign in with:**
- GitHub
- GitLab
- Email/Password

Visit carrelapp.com for the web version with full feature parity.

## Data Safety Questionnaire Answers

### Data Collection
- **Email address**: Collected for account creation
- **Name**: Collected from OAuth provider for display
- **Photos**: Profile picture URL from OAuth provider
- **App activity**: Collected for error tracking
- **Files and documents**: PDFs cached locally

### Data Sharing
- No data is shared with third parties for advertising
- Data is shared with Convex (backend provider) for app functionality

### Security
- Data encrypted in transit (HTTPS)
- Authentication tokens encrypted at rest
- Users can request data deletion

### Data Deletion
- Users can delete their account from the app
- All personal data deleted within 30 days of account deletion
